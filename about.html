About me.

I'm Leanalyn — Lea, if that's easier. I'm a senior at Benedictine University finishing a B.S. in Computer Information Systems with a minor in Mathematics, and I'm based in the Chicago suburbs, where I've lived my whole life.

01 The path here

I started at College of DuPage, where I earned my Associate of Arts in May 2024. CoD is where I figured out how to actually be a college student — how to study, how to ask for help, how to show up. I transferred to Benedictine to finish my bachelor's degree, and I'm on track to graduate in December 2026.

After that, I'm planning to start an MBA in Spring 2027. I'm also a registered candidate with the Society of Actuaries, currently studying for Exam FM — math has always been the subject I trust the most, so it made sense to point it toward something concrete.

Right now I work as a Front Desk Receptionist in the President's Office at Benedictine, which means I spend my days doing the kind of detail-heavy, people-facing work that doesn't show up on a transcript but probably shaped how I think as much as any class did.
