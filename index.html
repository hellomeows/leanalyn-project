Capstone, 2026

Hi, I'm Leanalyn.

I'm a senior at Benedictine University finishing up a B.S. in Computer Information Systems with a minor in Mathematics. This site is my capstone project, basically a slightly more honest version of a portfolio: not a highlight reel, just a record of what I actually worked on and learned along the way.

I like problems that have exact answers. That's probably the clearest way to describe why I ended up studying both computer science and math instead of picking one. A lot of my coursework sits right at that intersection, databases, link analysis, cost accounting, calculus, and I've written more about all of it on the coursework page.

The more interesting part of this site, at least to me, is the tools page. I built three small calculators from concepts I actually studied: a time value of money calculator, a Newton's Method visualizer, and a tiny PageRank simulator. None of them are big, but they all actually run the math instead of just describing it, which felt like a better way to prove I learned something than just listing a class name.

If you want the non-academic version of me, Japanese culture and anime, Vocaloid, history, philosophy, that's mostly on the about page. And if you just need the short, scannable version of all of this, there's a normal resume too.

Thanks for reading this far. Feel free to look around.

— Lea
